Rafael's blog
Posted February 15

What are you going to do after you graduate from high school? If you don't have a clue or feel totally confused, don't worry! Today's blog is about the skills you and I are going to need when it's our turn to look for work.

Technology is changing everything, right? It's taking over some jobs but creating others. Young people with I.T. skills are probably going to find jobs easily. They're going to be in charge of all the technology of the future. My friend Blanca wants to have this type of job. She wants to be a programmer or a web designer, but that's not for me. I'm not going to look for a job in I.T. I want to do something different.

Thinking skills, like critical thinking and problem solving, are already really important for a lot of jobs these days. They'll probably be even more important in the future. Doctors, lawyers, and business people use them a lot. My friend Ashley wants to be a lawyer. She's going to study law in college.

For me, the most important skills are going to be communication skills, things like writing and public speaking. You need these skills for a lot of careers, especially if you want to be a teacher, a manager, or to work in the media.

And that's what I want to do. I'm going to be a journalist. I love writing about all kinds of different things. To be honest, that's why I like writing my blog. That's it for today. Hope this was useful. See you next time!
